Game Modes:
	Simplicity
		- basic gems only, simple time limit, strive for the top score
	Contingency
		- basic gems and time gems, extend time limit for as long as possible
	Insanity
		- all gems, special gems in profuse amounts, strive for top score
	Progressivity
		- basic and special gems, different levels with progressively larger score goals
	Velocity
		- basic gems only, shorter time limit, strive for top score
	Totality
		- basic and special gems, no time limit, board doesn't refill with gems, eliminate as many gems as possible
